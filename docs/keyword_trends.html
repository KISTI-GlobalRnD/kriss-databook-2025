Interactive plot of top-ranked keywords by research cluster and metric.

Keyword Trend Explorer / 키워드 추세 탐색기

Interactively inspect how the highest-ranked keywords evolve across years for each research cluster. Use the selectors to switch between clusters and metrics while the legend lets you toggle individual keyword curves on and off. All data are sourced from the comprehensive keyword_all_timeseries export generated after canonical integration.

Tip

모든 곡선은 문헌 비중(ppm_series_val, parts-per-million)을 나타냅니다. 포인트에 마우스를 올리면 각 용어의 score(c‑TF‑IDF)와 frequency(문헌 수)를 함께 확인할 수 있습니다.

Document Share (PPM) · Parts per million of the cluster’s total unigram tokens for each term-year pair. ( _{c,y}(t) = 10^{6} )

Score · Cluster-specific c‑TF‑IDF weight used to rank keywords (constant across years).

Frequency · Count of documents within the cluster that contain the keyword (constant across years).

Both Score and Frequency appear inside the hover tooltips for each point.
